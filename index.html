2D RHD Simulations: Shock Breakout in SN 1987A-like BSG

Chen, W.-Y., et al. | 2024 | The Astrophysical Journal

Multi-group FLD simulations exploring breakout signatures and early light-curve diagnostics. (Click to view summary)

ApJ (IOPscience)
Summary
  • Goal: Model SBO from SN 1987A-like BSG in 2D and quantify early LCs.
  • Method: CASTRO + MGFLD, OPAL opacities; angle-integrated & angle-resolved LCs.
  • Key findings: Viewing-angle dependence of peak L and rise time; diffusion→free-streaming imprints color-T; multi-group essential.

Preprint: Shock Breakout in Red Supergiants with Confined CSM

Chen, W.-Y., et al. | 2025 | In Prep.

2D RHD of SBO in RSG progenitors with confined circumstellar shells. (Click to view summary)

arXiv Preprint
Summary
  • Goal: Explore shock breakout in RSGs surrounded by confined CSM shells.
  • Method: CASTRO 2D MGFLD with OPAL opacities; variation in CSM density and thickness.
  • Key findings: CSM strongly delays breakout and enhances luminosity; multi-D instabilities imprint observable LC differences.
  • Implication: Distinguishes confined-shell environments from steady winds; informs early SN survey strategies.
